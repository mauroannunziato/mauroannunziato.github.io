Tao-interazione

Annunziato, Gemma de Julio, Pierucci, '96. Interactive installation

This installation is based on the dynamical interaction between the observer and a multivision system, in which counteracting couples of images and musical compositions are presented. The observer-listener could navigate by walking on the performance room, in order to determine the superposition degree between the two sound-visual stymulus. Therefore he can experience the modification of his perception based on his movement.

Lago sotteraneo

Annunziato, '94. Installation.

Underground lake where fantasy and memory merge together. A digital image is horizontally projected on a sheet of water in a natural cave (exhibited in a cave in Bassano in Teverina). The play of vibrations of waves and the reflections on the wall recalls the typical dynamics of memory images.

Il te'nel deserto

Annunziato, Gemma de Julio '96. Installation

A vision stolen from a dream, or a memory.

A sequence of images is projected on a painting in the dark. The images are digital elaborated versions of the painting itself. The effect is a strong colour and edge vibrations continuosly changing the the colour and the emotional impact of the vision.
