Vibrations

Mauro Annunziato, '94-'96. Digital Images
The images of this series are build on the base of the simulation of vibration and interference phenomena like the acustic vibrations of music and sounds in a reflecting environment. The creative power of these simulations consists in the visualizing phenomena which are normally around our life (probably perceived) but not visible. The inspiration is the ancient myth of the man of the third eye of perception.
