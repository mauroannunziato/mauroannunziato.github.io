Collections of digital prints (M. Annunziato; media: paper, wood, marble; limited copies)

 

 

Artificial Societies

This collection of about 50 images are inspired to the creation of artificial societies, reflections of the real social dynamics. They are produced creating a virtual world in the computer where digital beings live, reproduce, evolve...

 

Kaos

Kaos is a collection of about 400 images realized in '94-'96. The images are inspired to the chaos theory as an incredible source of creative spatio-temporal structures. Several methods of realization have been used in order to explore the chaos border...

 

 

Relazioni Emergenti

These images (about 30 images) come from the interactive installation "Relazioni Emergenti" ('2000) presented in many festivals of emergent art. They are created directly interacting in the installation in sinergy with the musics produced by the filaments.

 

 

Night Visions

This collection ('00-'01) is composed by images inspired to the night visions. ...in the night the objects change the color lighten by the immagination...
