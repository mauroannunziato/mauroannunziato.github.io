Contaminazione

 

Realization: '99, Resolution: 4000x12000 pixels. It represents the creative contamination between two lineage: order and chaos, synthesis and complexity, east and west. The fusion of the two species generates the most creative zone, the "edge of chaos", through the genetic fusion of the two characters. This image has been chosen by NASA-JPL to be sent on the first human village on Mars in 2030. (see in www.planetary.org\scien). So this will be the Plancton most travelling image !

Chaos Revenge

 

Realization: '99, Resolution: 3000x9000 pixels. This image is the mine most known. Selected at Siggraph '99 (LA, USA) was reported on the Art Catalogue front cover and in the Press Show Highlights. Since '99 has been included in a traveling show for three years along exhibitions on emergent art in USA. It is the revenge of chaos over the order through the power of create new shapes of real or artificial life. It is curious that many people looking this image don't believe it is made using a computer.

Metamorfosi

Realization: '99, Resolution: 3000x9000 pixels. This is one of my preferred images for the social implications and for the concept of the aesthetics of the biodiversity. The evolution come from left. Trough the space colonization some pioneers (straight lines) act as a sort of containment. The subsequent development creating cultural islands on the right. This mechanism create many local zones of different evolution developing complexity and biodiversity.

Infolife

 

Realization: '2000, Resolution: 10.000x40.000 pixels !!. This image has a huge resolution, impossible to treat with little computers and commercial screens. It is difficult to render with prints under 1.5 m. I tried to push all the computing power I had in order to enlarge the evolution. I was able to see the image only several days after the evolution start. At the end an incredible world rich of microcosmos and different local societies is emerged. And you can walk through the evolution...
