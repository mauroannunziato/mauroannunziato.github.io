Interactive Installations

Sensitive Painting

Artists: M. Annunziato, I. Tirelli, P. Pierucci, '02

Sensitive Painting is an interactive installation where a projector linked to a computer, casts images on a large size picture representing the eternal flux between Eros and Thanatos. The images projected on the painting consist of the internal layers of the painting memorized in the computer. Before the projection, the images are modified in terms of saturation, hue, color inversion and other color contrast models in relation to the people interaction. The perceptive results depends on the composition of the projected image, and the reflection on the physical matter of the real painting. In front of the painting the visitors can interact on a "sensitive woman surface". Moving their hands on the 3D surface the people induce a reaction from the painting taht reacts modifying the perceptive variables and modulating a sound flux that appears to be produced by the painting itself.

The installation has been exibhited in the Digital Art Gallery, Brunei Museum, London '02, High Tech Museum, fortezza da Basso, Florence '02.

Relazioni Emergenti

Artists: M. Annunziato, P. Pierucci, '99-'00

It is an environment of artificial life where the individuals, represented by graphics filaments, are endowed with own intelligence and character. They can reproduce, evolve and interact with real people. The observers can interact with the artwork itself transmitting "energy and life" to the living filaments which start to compose music related to their genetic structure.

The installation has been exibhited in the main international festival on electronic emergent art (Imagina '00 Monaco; Siggraph '00 New Orleans; Opera Totale '99, Venice , Monumedia '00, Naples, Virtuality '00 Turin, Cinema '99, Sorrento, Generative Art '99, Milan.

Vid-A-Feeba

Artists: M. Annunziato, P. Pierucci, '99

An interactive play with a chaotic process. The images follow the iterated evolution typical of chaotic systems showing quick change in the visual and sound patterns. The movement of the visitors inserts disturbances that move the system toward the edge of chaos where new creative patterns emerge and the artwork re-creates itself.

Stanze

Artists: M. Annunziato, P. Pierucci, O. Gemma de Julio, '96

An interactive installation where the observer is projected in the artist rooms like in a virtual set. He can navigate through the rooms and their symbols producing music with the movements of his body. The natural gesture is embodied in musical and visual effects, so the visitor tends to losses the consciousness to drive the installation itself.
