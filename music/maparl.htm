Macchine Parlanti

M.Giustiniani, P.Pierucci

Principles, history and technology of text to speech synthesis.
Franco Angeli, Milano, 1990.

The development of information theory and computer technology still left unsolved many questions concerning the way humans interact with machines. The voice seems one of the means through which it could be possible, hopefully in the near future, to achieve a direct and immediate communication with machines. This book is about the history and the technology of text to speech systems, that is the systems that are able to translate in intelligible voice any written text.
