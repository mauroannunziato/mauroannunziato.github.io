An artificial life environment for solution

development and process optimization

M. Annunziato, S. Pizzuti (ENEA, Italy)

P. Pierucci (IRIS, Italy)

Lev Tsimring (UCSD, USA)

This paper reports some applications of artificial life environments to the development of solutions based on the evolutionary properties. We show how this approach is able to create complex structures and how they can be used to solve optimization problems and applied to the process control and optimization.
