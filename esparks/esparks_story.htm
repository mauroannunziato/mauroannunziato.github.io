The E-SPARKS story

(M. Annunziato, P.Pierucci, 2000-2006)

 

E-sparks is a long story of ideas, experiments and backstage exploration. We started with the first idea and draft at the beginning of 2000. From 2000 to 2006 several times the target were changed and the prototypes. But we didn't change the Artist Statement and the original structure of the project.

Difficulties arised everywhere: framework, aesthetics, technologies, scientific references, interaction paradigms, funding. It has been hard to combine all these elements, but it has been a really formative experience walking in the dark..

 

2000

What does it means "communication" ?

This was the starting point of the E-Sparks idea: connect humans and artificials. The problem was to define which kind of communication (bio-chemical, symbolic, semantic). Which kind of symbols ? What does a symbol is ? from which step of evolution we have to start ?

We started obscillating between the refrence of the ant and the dog. At the end we decided it make no sense take an animal as a reference. We preferred call them just creatures, something of alien from human/animal nature but reflecting something of the human nature.

 

2001

Learn to eat to survive !

The first backstage experiment was involved in the development of a artificial life environment with a society of evolving creatures. The creatures were equipped with a neural network controlling the movement. During the evolution they were able to develop ability to search and eat food bits to survive. See the paper "The Emergence of Social Learning in Artificial Societies" for a detailed description. The first shape of creatures was a simple cube.

Aurora di Venere

"Aurora di Venere" was a performance represented in an important italian theatre (Palais de San Vincent). A group of dancers interacted with digital entities projected over two screens located at the background and at the front (semi-transparent) of the theatre's stand. It was also an important source of funds for the continuation of the E-Sparks project. Go to the "aurora di venere" to have more info.

 

2002

Search for a language

In the second backstage experiment the society was able to develop autonomously a common primitive language in terms of the more clear set of symbols used to express a specific content. This experiment refers to the idea of the evolutionary theory of the development of language. To have more details about this experiment you can dowload the paper "Human-Artificial Ecosystems: Searching for a Language". The combination of individual learning and social information sharing was really surprising.

The voice of the alien

In the first attemptatives to create speaking creature we tried to implement a progressive learning mechanism. This mechanism apply a morphing between two words. The results was a sort of robotic voice difficult to manipulate and understand. The creatures were interesting but not very friendly. The personification of the artificial creature was difficult. The problem was that the creature had a throat that was too different from us !!

 

("fish & cat", 6.3 Mb)

 

2004

Singing, why not !

After the robotic voice experiment, we were trying other roads. Searching for an intermediate solution we used a pitch-to-MIDI converter in order to capture the tonal trend of the voice of the visitors and convert in creature language. This experiment was very interesting: the visitors try to teach the creatures to sing a note sequence or a musical phrase. It was an intersting experiment, but the musical sequence were difficult to recognize and difficult to use for communication. They lack in the semantics.

This prototype was exhibited at the Italian conference on Artificial Life, Rome, March 05

Interacting with the human shadow

A new paradigm for interaction was implemented. It was based on the shadow of the visitors over the screen. the creatures follow the human shadow !!

 

Voice, voice, they can speak !!!

Finally Piero developed a voice throat model and an alghoritm for word/sentences classification based on elastic matching. This aspect opened a lot of possibilities in terms of manipulating the human voice. They can speak using the real visitor voice or the synthetic one.

 

Dialog & fiction

The last step was the development of a learning mechanism in order to search for word/sentences free association (generally is a semantic link). The idea is simple: if you give me an answer to my word I suppose a semantic link could exists between the two words. If this is reinforced by many visitor I became sure that a link exist. In this way after some weeks of interaction with the people each creature build own language network. Then they try to use the language networks to create a fiction of dialog with the people. I have to say they are very intriguing and provocative sometime...

the language semantic network of a creature after a week of life

 

E-Sparks life started this day !!!

E-Sparks official birth is 14 September 05. In this day the e-sparks society started to life in the fantastic framework of the ancient roman imperial forum (Mercati of Traiano). This exhibition is two months long with about 3500-4000 visitors/week starting from the first visitor, the Major of Rome Walter Veltroni. Since that day the creatures are interacting, living and learning each day something more of the italian language and of the romans dialectics. Sometime some creature go out from the computer in the night looking the moon over the roman ruins, but this is another story...

2006

Oopps !!!

After the 50.000 visitors at the exibhition of Mercati of Traiano, the E-sparks life changed dramatically. The people have inserted intense human characters in the creatures (vocalisms, words, multilanguages sentences, emotions) and a new question strongly emerge: are the creatures external to us or they are they FRAGMENTS OF OUR MINDS that interact togheter ? The sequence of sentences seems a story of the building of a COLLECTIVE DREAM.

The MART and the collective dream

In august 2006 we have been invited to exhibit Esparks at MART (Musem of Contemporary Art of Trento and Rovereto). We would like to stress the idea of the collective story-telling and the collective dream. For this reason, in this version the creatures are less visible and they are immerged in evocative images to stimyulate the building of stories from the visitors. Standing and dialoging in the installation, sometimes you think about artificial creatures, other time you can imagine to speak to alien fragments of your mind.
