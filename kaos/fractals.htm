Fractals

Mauro Annunziato, '94-'96. Digital Images

The fractals are the most known images inspired to the chaos. Its discovery, by Mandelbrot, has open the way for many experimentations both in scientific and aesthetic worlds. Two of the most interesting features of the fractals are the incredible complexity and the self-similarity, that is the similarity between the global and the local structures mantained at different observation scales.
