Cellular Automata

Mauro Annunziato, '94-'96. Digital Images
The cellular automata are evolution models of biological population based on simple laws for the reproduction of the local tissue. The interconnected behaviour of the population can reveal very complex global constructions and organizations. This mechanism is on the base of many biological systems. Depending on the features of the local growing laws, it is possible reach very interesting images on the border between order and chaos.
