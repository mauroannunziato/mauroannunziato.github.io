Attractors

Mauro Annunziato, '94-'96. Digital Images

The attractors represent the synthesis of the dynamics of a system, that is the evolution through different configurations of the system. The charm of attractors is the property to point out the hidden dynamic structures which are not easily observable looking for separate states apparently not correlated. The goal is to capture the essence of the movement or the behaviour in order to reconstruct the evolution story of the system.
