Iterated Evolutions

Mauro Annunziato, '94-'96. Digital Images

The images of the Iterated Evolution series are inspired to the dynamic of the transformations of systems subjected to an iterated evolution law. This concept is expressed through the iterated application of little trasformations which are able to change, along the evolution life, the features of the starting image. Tipically I use real images as starting point and neural networks for the transformation law in order to evoke some aspects of the image processing in the human mind.
