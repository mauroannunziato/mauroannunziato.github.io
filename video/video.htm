Como avanza el pez

Annunziato, Gemma de Julio, Pierucci, '94, Video, 7'20".

A play of echoes of a fish moving across a painting which represents the same fish. The painted element and the fish move together on a TV screen trough a video-feedback chaotic loop. They are linked with sounds recalling the bounded reality of a communication-containment tool.

Trains don't run forever

Annunziato, Gemma de Julio, Pierucci, '95. Video, 8'. Slide-projection.

Three old men of Bassano in Teverina, a small village close to Rome, describe an historical event: in 1943 the Germans bombed a train carrying explosives and the small village was destroyed. This image of a past war is interpreted by a modern observer facing the lack of historical memory of present time, in which new forms of violence arise. Digital images alternate with paintings and sounds of the past and of the present.

Interno caotico

Annunziato, De Julio, Pierucci, '96. Video, 7'. Slide-projection.

Sounds, artificial and painted images in a continuous overlapping produce a full immersion in the world of chaotic creativity. Notes, icons and random geometries fused and linked each other to suggest a more intense multimedia concept: the same soul showing itself through different media.
