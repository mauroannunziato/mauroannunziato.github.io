Patterns

Mauro Annunziato, '94-'96. Digital Images

The images of the Patterns series are the result of the chaotic superimposition of a multitude of geometric elements. The global interaction creates structures of shapes and textures very different from the single geometric element. The chaotic laws utilized for the localization of the elements are strongly affected by boundary (non linear) constraints. The results is the emerging of a principle of aesthetic self-organization of the element population. The images are obtained as spatio-temporal windows of the superimposition process on a infinite pseudo-spherical tissue.
